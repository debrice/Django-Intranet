Jan 14, 2010 8:33 amTop Story

Pamela Stowers

Fulham Co, announces that Pamela Stowers will take the position of Marketing Communications Manager.  Fulham has identified the need for a full time marketing person to coordinate and execute trade shows, the website, catalogs and literature as well as advertising and promotions.  As Marketing Communications Manager, she will also be responsible for the handling of Fulham’s promotional and instructional video program.  Most importantly, Pamela will provide support to Product and Channel Managers as well as Sales with polished marketing tools.  She will spend the next couple of weeks meeting with Managers to review programs and identify high priority marketing needs.

Pamela has 12 years of Marketing Communications experience, of which 8 years are in the lighting industry.

Pamela previously held the position of Lamp and LED Product Manager at Fulham.  The Product Management duties will be assumed by a yet unidentified individual. In the interim, Brian Wald will handle questions and issues concerning LEDs.

Please congratulate Pamela on her new position and watch for the exciting new Marketing tools to come.

  • Dec 15, 2009 9:29 amFULHAM Year End Inventory

    Fouad is the Head of the Fulham Inventory Committee. Quote: “Year-end inventory date has been finalized, Fulham inventory is scheduled for Monday, January 4th. All employees are expected to be present on inventory days - unless there has been a pre-approved absence. Everyone participates in the inventory counts in one way or another. Some people will stay in the office to handle phone calls, but most everyone will be assigned to a counting team. Individual counters will be able to conduct their inventory count and return to the office to work on their jobs, but they may be called back to re-count as needed later in the day. Assignments will be issued before the inventory date. Although we strive to be as efficient and quick as possible with our counts, there is always the possibility that employees need to stay late in order to complete the inventory. Since the inventory is conducted in the warehouse, blue jeans, closed-toed shoes (tennis shoes are okay) and warm clothing are highly recommended!”
